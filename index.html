Life Processes

Life Process:-For a living being it is necessary to perform basic functions for proper body fuctioning,so that it can prevent damage & breakdown of body & its functions.These basic functions performed by organism to maintain life are known as life processes.

Nutrition

It is the process of transferingg source of energy (food) from outside to inside the body of an organisms for maintaining living structure.

There are two types of Nutrition

1.Autotrophic Mode of Nutrition

2.Heterotrophic Mode Of Nutrition

Photosynthesis:-

It is a complex process by which green parts of plant synthesis organic food in form of glucose from carbondioxide & water in presence of sunlight and chlorophyll.

Respiration -

It is a process by wwhich food is oxydised to release energy .For this Oxygen is required from ouside of the body to breakdown the food.The organic substance undergoing oxydative breakdown during respiration are called respiratory Substrate.Eg.Glucose

Transportation-

It is a life process in which a substance absorbed is transported from one body part to its other part.

Excretion -

It is biological process by which an organism removes harmful metabolic waste from the body.
