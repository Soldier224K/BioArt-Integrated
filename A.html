Autotrophic Mode Of Nutrition

It is mode of nutrition in which organisms ,mainly green plants manufacture thier food by itself.This mode of nutrition is performed by organisms called autotrophs.

There are two types of autotrophs:-

1)Photosynthesis Autotrophs-Organisms which fulfil thier carbon and energy requirements by process of photosynthesis using sunlight as a source of energy. Eg.Green plants

2)Chemosynthesis Autotrohs:-Organisms which utilise chemical energy to sythesis their organic material(food). Eg.Bacteria
